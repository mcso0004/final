History

Northern Expedia was founded in 2007 in order to fill in the vacuum that is northern travel, with many people choosing to vacation to expensive, tropical locations, but we recognize that not everyone enjoys the sweltering heat of the southern hemisphere, instead we chose to create and provide one of the most diverse travel services, catering to those who prefer a different vacation experience, who might wish to see the many sights and enjoy the many things the northern hemisphere has to offer.
